You can be confident that by choosing to buy FPV drone components from us, you will receive products that meet all modern safety and efficiency standards.

Wide Selection of FPV Quadcopter Parts

We offer a wide range of parts for FPV quadcopters to meet diverse pilot needs. From maintenance to upgrading your drone - we have everything necessary to ensure smooth operation of your FPV drone. Our assortment includes components from leading manufacturers, such as:

  • Frames and motors from BETAFPV
  • Antennas and flight controllers from Happymodel
  • Propellers and batteries from GEPRC
  • Cameras from RunCam
  • Video transmitters and receivers from TBS (Team BlackSheep)

Components and spare parts for FPV drones are an integral part of any drone building, upgrading, or repair process. As the FPV drone market constantly evolves, we regularly update our assortment to offer you the opportunity to buy the latest and highest quality components for your FPV quadcopter.

Motors and Propellers

One of the most important elements of an FPV drone is motors and propellers. They are responsible for providing lift and movement of the drone in the air. When choosing motors and propellers, it's important to pay attention to their power, size, and compatibility with other drone components. Additionally, it's recommended to choose motors and propellers that provide an optimal balance between flight time and speed.

Flight Controllers

Flight controllers are the brain of an FPV drone, responsible for stability and flight control. It receives data from various sensors and makes decisions about controlling each motor. When choosing a flight controller, it's important to consider its compatibility with connected components such as radio receivers and sensors.

Video Camera and Video Transmitter

FPV flights require a video camera and video transmitter that allow pilots to see the surrounding environment in real-time. The video camera should have high resolution and minimal latency for optimal flight experience. When choosing a transmitter, it's important to consider its power and compatibility with FPV goggles or monitors.

Batteries and Chargers

Batteries are the power source for FPV drones and affect flight duration. When choosing batteries, it's important to pay attention to their capacity, voltage, and charging connectors. Chargers should be compatible with the chosen battery models and provide fast and safe charging.
