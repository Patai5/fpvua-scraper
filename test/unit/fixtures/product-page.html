The Axisflying case makes installation easier and improves the performance of the DJI O4 Air Unit system.

The heatsink provides constant ventilation thanks to its aluminum construction and protects the DJI O4 Air Unit from dirt and mechanical damage. Dimensions: 33.5mm * 33.5mm * 9mm, weight: 8.7g, mounting pattern: 25.5mm * 25.5mm (M2).

The DJI O4 camera housing allows you to securely mount the camera on an FPV drone frame for stable and smooth footage during flight. Thanks to its compact size and 14mm and 20mm mounting holes, this part offers high reliability, flexibility, and impact resistance, providing solid protection for the camera.
